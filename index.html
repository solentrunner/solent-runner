🔒 Privacy Policy

📋 What Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to fulfil your delivery request: your name, phone number / WhatsApp, pickup address, delivery address, item details, and preferred contact method. We do not collect payment card details, national identity numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to process your delivery request, provide a price quote, and coordinate the delivery. When you submit a booking, your details are transmitted securely via EmailJS (an email delivery service) and stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Solent Runner. No automated profiling or marketing activities are carried out using your data.

🤝 Third Parties

We use EmailJS (emailjs.com) and Google Sheets / Google Workspace solely as tools to receive and store booking data. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party for commercial purposes. Both EmailJS and Google are GDPR-compliant processors operating under their own privacy frameworks.

⏱ How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your booking data only for as long as reasonably necessary — typically up to 12 months for accounting and service record purposes, in accordance with UK HMRC guidance for sole traders. After this period, records are securely deleted.

⚖️ Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. You may also object to processing or request data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

📞 Contact & Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data or raise a privacy concern, please contact Ming Chak Lai (Data Controller, Solent Runner) via WhatsApp at 07566 822817. We will respond within 30 days in line with UK GDPR requirements.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We only collect data voluntarily submitted via our booking form.

🔄 Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our operations or legal obligations. The current version is always available on this page. Last updated: April 2026.
